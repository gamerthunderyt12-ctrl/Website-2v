03

Craftsmanship, step by step.

A Muskan Furniture piece takes eight quiet, deliberate steps — from the first pencil line on plywood to the final wipe of polish on site.

01

Site measurement

Laser + hand-check on every wall, ceiling and skirting. Nothing gets ordered until millimetres are agreed.

02

Design & 3D preview

You see the exact bedroom, kitchen or wardrobe in 3D before a single sheet is cut.

03

Precision cutting

BWP ply is CNC-cut and edge-banded in our workshop, not the field.

04

Assembly & hardware

Blum / Hettich hinges, soft-close channels and screwed carcasses — no nails, no glue-only joints.

05

Finish & polish

PU, acrylic, veneer or laminate — sealed and buffed to a mirror-quiet finish.

06

Installation on site

Installed by our own team. No sub-contractors on your walls.

07

Quality walk-through

Every drawer opened, every door aligned, every LED checked — with you present.

08

Handover & warranty

Written warranty on hardware & carcass. We answer the phone after handover.
